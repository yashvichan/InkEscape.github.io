Cursed Memories

Scene: Two teachers in the staff room

The dimly lit staff room was quiet, save for the ticking of a clock on the wall. Ren Tsubaki’s voice was low, almost a whisper.

Ren Tsubaki: "Time doesn’t change anything... Some things remain burned into our memory."

Hikaru Sugawara: (nodding) "No matter how much we try to bury them, some things refuse to stay forgotten."

Scene: A new transfer student arrives

The new student stood at the front of the room, feeling the weight of dozens of eyes on him.

Shin Kurosawa: "Good morning, everyone. I am Shin Kurosawa. I hope to get along with all of you."

Their homeroom teacher, Ren Tsubaki, smiled.

Ren Tsubaki: "Everyone, give a warm welcome to your new classmate."

Scene: At lunchtime, Shin is surrounded by students

At lunch, a crowd gathered around Shin, bombarding him with questions.

  • "What was your previous school like?"
  • "Do you miss your old classmates?"
  • "Why did you come to such a small town from a big city like Tokyo?"
  • "What are your hobbies?"

Shin: "I moved here because my dad got transferred. He left me with my grandparents. I don’t have any particular hobbies, but I miss some old classmates. I like this town because I want to be close to nature, which isn't possible in Tokyo."

Scene: A conversation with Ema

Later, after school, Shin spotted Ema alone and approached her.

Shin: "Why did you look so worried when I asked about the school? Is there something I should know?"

Ema: (hesitating) "Listen, there’s something you need to know... about this school. Every fourteen days, someone dies—a student, or a teacher."

Scene: The realization

Days passed, and the ominous weight of Ema's words lingered in Shin's mind. As the deadline approached, whispers in the hallways grew louder.

Shin approached Hiroshi again.

Shin: "Do you believe Ema’s story? About the curse?"

Hiroshi: (somber) "She’s not wrong. I’ve heard the rumours... but I thought they were just stories to scare new students."

Scene: Discovering the Body

They followed the sound of a scream to a secluded corner of the schoolyard, where a small group of students had gathered, their faces pale and terrified.

Shin pushed through the crowd, his stomach knotting as he spotted the source of the commotion.

Tomoya: (horrified) "It’s Hana Suzuki… from the calligraphy club."

Scene: Confronting the Librarian

Shin and Ema hurried to the library.

Shin: "We need to speak with Keigo-sensei. Maybe he knows something."

Keigo-sensei: (dark expression) "Missing pages, you say? I’ve heard of those… But I’m not sure you’ll like what you find."

Scene: The Curse’s Reach

Airi tried to escape, but as she and her family left town, a storm raged.

The next morning, whispers spread through the school: Airi and her family had died in a tragic accident.

"They say she was trying to escape the curse," one student muttered.

"But no one can escape it… it doesn’t let you leave," another replied, shivering.
